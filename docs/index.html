Welcome to XTRA PR71 Router Controller

An Open-Source Project Alpha v0.1.0

The ultimate tool for managing your XTRA PR71 pocket router with ease and efficiency. This is an independent project not affiliated with the manufacturers of XTRA PR71 devices.

Key Features

Easy Setup

Configure your router with just a few taps. No technical knowledge required.

Device Management

View and manage all devices connected to your router network.

Network Statistics

Monitor data usage, signal strength, and connection quality in real-time.

SMS Management

Send and receive SMS messages directly through your router's SIM card.

Why Choose Our App?

Our open-source app provides a user-friendly interface for complete control of your XTRA PR71 router. With robust security features and a focus on privacy, you can manage your network with confidence.
